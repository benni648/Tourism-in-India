**Tourism in Assam** is a vibrant mix of natural beauty, cultural heritage, and wildlife, making it one of the most sought-after destinations in northeastern India. The state is famous for its lush tea gardens, scenic landscapes, and the mighty **Brahmaputra River**, which offers opportunities for river cruises and cultural exploration. Assam is home to the world-renowned **Kaziranga National Park**, a UNESCO World Heritage site known for its population of one-horned rhinoceroses. The state also boasts rich Assamese culture, reflected in its classical dance forms like **Sattriya**, traditional festivals like **Bihu**, and temples such as the **Kamakhya Temple**. From adventure seekers to cultural enthusiasts, Assam offers a wide array of experiences, making it a captivating destination for all kinds of travelers.
