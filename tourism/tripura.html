Tourism in **Tripura** offers a unique blend of natural beauty, historical sites, and cultural heritage, making it an ideal destination for travelers seeking both tranquility and adventure. The state is known for its stunning landscapes, including lush green hills, waterfalls, and serene lakes. Key attractions include the majestic **Ujjayanta Palace** in Agartala, the only **Neermahal Water Palace**, and the sacred **Tripura Sundari Temple** in Udaipur, one of the 51 Shakti Peethas. Nature lovers can explore the **Sepahijala Wildlife Sanctuary**, home to diverse flora and fauna, or trek through the scenic **Jampui Hills**. The state is also rich in archaeological heritage, with sites like **Chabimura** and **Unakoti**, known for their ancient rock carvings.
