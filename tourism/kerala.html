Tourism in Kerala is a captivating blend of natural beauty, rich cultural heritage, and unique experiences. Known as "God's Own Country," Kerala attracts visitors with its lush landscapes, tranquil backwaters, golden beaches, hill stations, and vibrant wildlife. The state is renowned for its serene houseboat cruises through the backwaters of Alleppey and Kumarakom, the misty tea gardens of Munnar, and the wildlife sanctuaries of Thekkady and Wayanad. Kerala also offers a rich cultural tapestry, from ancient temples and historic forts in places like Fort Kochi and Trivandrum to the world-famous Kathakali dance and Ayurveda therapies. With a tropical climate that appeals to travelers year-round, Kerala offers a diverse range of experiences, from adventure trekking in the Western Ghats to sunbathing on the pristine beaches of Kovalam and Varkala.
