Tourism in **Uttar Pradesh** offers a vibrant mix of historical, cultural, and spiritual experiences, making it one of the most popular tourist destinations in India. The state is home to some of the country's most iconic landmarks, including the majestic **Taj Mahal** in Agra, a UNESCO World Heritage site and one of the Seven Wonders of the World. Uttar Pradesh is also known for its deep spiritual significance, with **Varanasi**, one of the oldest living cities in the world, drawing millions of pilgrims to its ghats on the **Ganges River**. The state is rich in Mughal and Hindu architecture, with attractions such as **Fatehpur Sikri**, **Agra Fort**, and the sacred **Kashi Vishwanath Temple**. From the vibrant festivals of **Mathura** and **Vrindavan**, known for their connection to Lord Krishna, to the historic sites of **Sarnath** and **Kushinagar**, Uttar Pradesh is a haven for those seeking to explore India's spiritual roots and diverse heritage.
